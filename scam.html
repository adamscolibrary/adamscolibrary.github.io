⚠️ Scam Alert: Fake Government Grant Scam

Beware of Fake Facebook Page: "Agent David Gilbert"

This individual is impersonating a government official, claiming to offer government grants of up to $50,000.

Victims are directed to contact "Agent David Gilbert" via Facebook Messenger and are asked to provide personal and financial information.

This is a scam. There is no legitimate government program offering grants in this manner. Do not share any personal information or send money to this individual.
